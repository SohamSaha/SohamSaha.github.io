Political Opinions in Turkey


The purpose of the project is to get a very general overview of the political opinions in Turkey. The current generation cares a great deal about politics, but only politics that occurs at the forefront of the news. Any other political opinions are almost instantly forgotten once a new thing pops up (minus the people who the politics directly affect). The visualization is a good way for people to remember that there are other countries that are dealing with their own issues and that these people have opinions as well.
